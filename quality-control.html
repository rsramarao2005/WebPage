Quality Control

GoCibo and quality go hand in hand. Here, we believe in putting in every single effort to match your expectations, please your taste buds and give you the best of customer service possible. This is exactly what makes us what we are! Our Home Chefs are the 'lifelines' of GoCibo and life here is all about 'hand to mouth' - but in a slightly different perspective. For us, life starts flowing from our Home Chefs' hands to reach our foodie customers' mouths! This is the reason we take utmost care in selecting our 'hands', our 'lifelines', our 'roots' - our Home Chefs!

Mentor and Training

We get to your plate, homemade food cooked by not just any chef, but specifically selected ones who are verified, approved, certified and HACCP trained by Hotel Management Professionals, here at GoCibo. Not just this, within 3 months of coming on-board, the GoCibo Home Chefs are assisted by GoCibo to apply for FSSAI licensing. Before bringing the Home Chefs on-board, not only their expertise and skill but also their kitchen, hygiene levels, equipment and utensils are put to a thorough test. After getting them on-board, knowledgeable food mentors at GoCibo mentor our Home Chefs with tips, tricks and cooking methods taught in the best of Hotel Management Schools.
