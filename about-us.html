About Us

The world can be divided into two types of people: Those who 'love to cook' and those who 'love to eat'. We, a bunch of Foodies from Hyderabad, put our foot down to bridge the distance between these two types of people and got them all under one roof - and that is how GoCibo was born! At GoCibo, we don't just cook homemade food and serve it with homemade love, we also test and taste the food prepared by our GoCibo home chefs to ensure it is prepared with perfection and quality right till the last morsel. Not just that, we eat it as well - because we believe in getting into our customer's shoes to know the real picture and most important of all we ensure that all our customers have a full stomach - be it in terms of food in the foodies' belly or bucks in the GoCibo home chefs' piggy-bank!
